Leon woke up on the ship to a very strange sound.

Not an alarm.

Not an explosion.

Just:

**"Prrrpffff-BLUP."**

Silence.

Then something squeaked.

Then a small cloud of green smoke came out of the ventilation.

Leon slowly opened his eyes.
— …oh no.

From the other end of the ship came the dragon's panicked voice:
— IT WASN'T ME!

Leon sat up.
— Dragon. The whole ship literally smells like burnt cheese and pancakes.

— …it was maybe a little me.

The Storygenerator flickered a warning.

— *ALERT: gas-dragon crisis.*

Leon put his face in his hands.
— Of course something like that exists.

---

The dragon sat hunched over his mini-campfire and looked very miserable.

— It's all because of the pancakes… — he moaned.

— How many did you eat? — Leon asked suspiciously.

The dragon started counting on his claws.

He counted for a long time.

A very long time.

Leon narrowed his eyes.
— Dragon…

— …thirty-two.

— THIRTY-TWO?!

— They were small!

Suddenly:

**PRRRROOOT.**

A small flame shot from under the dragon's tail and set fire to Leon's sock lying nearby.

— MY SOCK! — Leon yelped.

The dragon panicked.
— I'M SORRY! IT'S AUTOMATIC!

Storygenerator:
— *Detected: explosive digestion.*

— Thank you for the analysis, Sherlock — Leon muttered, putting out the sock.

---

Suddenly the ship shuddered.

— What now?! — Leon grabbed his seat.

The Storygenerator displayed a red warning:

— *Engines unstable.*

The dragon looked innocent.
— That's probably not because of me…

At that moment:

**BLUUUP-PFFFRRT.**

The ship accelerated sharply forward.

Leon nearly fell off his seat.
— DID YOU JUST PROPEL THIS SHIP WITH A FART?!

The dragon looked equally surprised.
— …maybe.

— I CANNOT BELIEVE THIS IS WORKING.

The Storygenerator flickered:
— *Emergency biological fuel active.*

Leon stared blankly ahead.
— I really need different adventures.

---

Suddenly an alarm beeped.

— *Warning: approaching Planet Gazozja.*

— That even sounds bad — said Leon.

They made an emergency landing.

The planet looked… strange.

Everywhere there were huge bubbles floating in the air.

And a lot of people holding their stomachs.

— Oh no… — the dragon whispered.

A small green alien ran up to them.

— Have YOU also got the Great Bloating?!

Leon looked at the dragon.
— …tell me this isn't your fault.

The dragon looked down.
— I may have given them the recipe for triple cheese-bean campfire pancakes…

Silence.

— YOU DID WHAT?!

— I thought they'd be happy!

At that moment a whole group of aliens released an enormous collective fart.

**PFFFFFFFFFFFRRRRRRRTTT.**

Dust rose into the air.

One bubble exploded.

Someone flew off on a chair.

Leon doubled over with laughter.
— No… I can't… breathe…

The dragon looked tragic.
— This is the worst day of my life.

— No. This is the best day of my life — Leon laughed.

---

— Right — he said at last, wiping tears from his eyes. — We have to fix this.

— How?!

Leon looked at the ingredients.

Beans.

Triple cheese.

Hot lava sauce.

— Dragon… that wasn't pancakes. That was a biological weapon.

— Oh.

The Storygenerator:
— *Analysis confirmed.*

Leon sighed.
— We need to make light pancakes.

The aliens groaned:
— But tastyyyy…

— They'll be tasty! Just without the explosions.

---

An hour later they were cooking new pancakes together.

Less cheese.

Zero lava sauce.

A few fruits.

The dragon watched suspiciously.
— This looks healthy.

— I know. Terrifying.

The aliens tried them.

Silence.

Leon tensed.
— Well?

The little alien gave a thumbs up.
— Doesn't hurt!

A second one:
— And nothing exploded!

A third one started crying with joy.
— I CAN SIT DOWN AGAIN!

The dragon let out a breath of relief.

Unfortunately literally.

**Prrt.**

A small flame shot from under his own tail and set it on fire.

— AAAA FLAMING BOTTOM! — the dragon screamed, running in circles.

Leon collapsed to the ground laughing.

The Storygenerator flickered:
— *Mission complete: gas levels stabilised.*

Leon was barely breathing from laughter.
— Dragon…

— Yes?

— Next time… you eat maximum five pancakes.

The dragon looked offended.
— That's inhumane.

— You're not even human!

— EXACTLY. I need more.

Leon just shook his head.

And somewhere in the background…

someone let off another cosmic fart.

And everyone instinctively jumped aside. 🐉🥞💨🚀✨
