Leon looked at the book he had brought back from the Zone of Silence.

On the cover it still read:
"Begin, even when it is quiet."

— Okay… but maybe something less… existential now — he muttered. — Maybe… pancakes?

The Storygenerator lit up immediately.

— Keyword detected: PANCAKES 🥞
— Adventure mode: CULINARY CRISIS

Leon burst out laughing.
— Finally, something serious.

Click.

The smell hit him first.

Warm. Sweet. Perfect.

— Oh yes… — Leon closed his eyes. — This is my environment.

He opened them.

He was standing in an enormous square. Around him were stalls, frying pans, flying pancakes and… crowds of people.

Above everything hung a huge banner:

"INTERGALACTIC PANCAKE FESTIVAL"

— Knew it — said Leon approvingly. — Space does make sense after all.

Suddenly someone ran past, crying:
— Disaster! It's a disaster!

Leon glanced at the Storygenerator.
— And here we go.

— Problem detected: a key ingredient is missing.

— Don't tell me…

— The Main Flavour has vanished.

Leon narrowed his eyes.
— …what does that mean?

He was quickly led to the central kitchen.

There, next to a giant frying pan, stood… a familiar dragon.

— YOU! — he cried. — The pancake one!

— Oh hey — Leon waved. — I see you've built a career.

— This is no time for jokes! — the dragon waved his tail dramatically. — The pancakes are… tasteless!

Leon picked up a piece.

It looked perfect.

He tried it.

He stopped.

— …alright. That's strange.

— Right?! — the dragon moaned.

— What exactly has gone missing? — Leon asked.

The Storygenerator displayed the data:
— Main Flavour = the thing that makes a pancake "someone's."

Leon raised an eyebrow.
— Meaning?

— It's not about ingredients. It's about the story.

Leon looked at the dragon.
— Tell me… who makes these pancakes?

— The finest chefs from across the galaxy!

Leon looked around.

Hundreds of perfect pancakes.

Every one identical.

— That's exactly it — he muttered.

He walked up to one of the chefs.

— How do you make your pancakes?

— By the recipe — came the stiff reply.

— And something of your own?
