That night aboard "Spark 7" everything was perfectly quiet.

Leon was sleeping soundly under a soft blanket.

The dragon was curled up beside him like a huge, gently snoring crescent roll.

The Storygenerator blinked quietly in the corner.

Everything was calm.

Until suddenly…

**PING!**

The whole ship blazed with pink light.

Leon opened his eyes.

And immediately knew that something was very wrong.

Everything was…

pink.

But not ordinary pink.

This was that absurdly intense shade of pink that made your eyes squint.

The walls were pink.

The floor pink.

Even the dragon was…

glittery pink.

He also had a large bow on his tail.

He looked at himself and screamed:

— LEON! I'M ADORABLE!

Leon looked at his own hands.

He was wearing a pink superhero jumpsuit.

With sequins.

And a cape.

On the chest in large letters:

**SUPER LEON PINK EDITION**

— Oh no, — he groaned.

The Storygenerator beeped cheerfully:

— *Welcome to PLANET PINK!*

The dragon beeped even louder.

— THIS IS THE MOST BEAUTIFUL PLACE IN THE UNIVERSE!

Click.

---

A second later they were standing in the middle of a city.

Everything was sweet.

Literally everything.

The houses looked like cupcakes.

The trees had leaves shaped like hearts.

The clouds looked like cotton candy.

Along the streets drove enormous **monster trucks**…

but pink, fluffy and decorated with bows.

Each one made a sound:

— *beeeep :3*

Leon looked around, bewildered.

— This is very strange.

The dragon had tears of joy in his eyes.

— This is perfect.

Suddenly a pink superhero swooped down from the sky.

He had a cape with frills.

And a glitter mask.

— Welcome! I am Captain Cupcake!

Leon sighed.

— Of course you are.

The captain looked dramatically into the distance.

— Planet Pink is in danger!

The dragon immediately stood up heroically.

— What's happened?!

The captain pointed at the horizon.

Approaching were…

monsters.

And zombies.

But they were cute too.

The zombies had pink bows.

The monsters had big sparkly eyes.

One was carrying a plush teddy bear.

Another was wearing a dress with little hearts.

But all of them roared menacingly:

— RAAAWR :3

Leon blinked.

— These might be the least scary monsters ever.

The captain trembled.

— Don't be fooled by their cute bows! They're dangerous!

The dragon stood in a fighting stance.

His bow rippled in the breeze.

— Leon. We have to save the planet.

Leon sighed.

— Obviously.

---

They jumped into their own monster truck.

It was gigantic.

Pink.

It had glitter flames along the sides.

And a huge bow on the roof.

The dragon squealed with delight.

— I WANT THIS FOREVER.

Leon started the engine.

The engine purred:

— *uwu*

— I don't believe this, — Leon muttered.

They roared off.

The monster truck bounced over sugary hills.

Zombies chased them waving their bows.

Monsters hurled glitter.

The dragon rolled down the window and bellowed:

— NOT TODAY, SWEET BEASTS!

He let out a battle toot.

**PRRT**

But instead of fire…

out came pink confetti.

The dragon froze.

— What?!

Again.

**PRRT**

More confetti.

Leon burst out laughing.

— The planet has cute-ified you!

— THIS IS AN OUTRAGE!

The monsters squealed with delight:

— He's so pretty!

The dragon looked like he was about to cry.

---

At last they reached a great castle made of cotton candy.

At the top stood King Pink.

He had a crown made of bows.

And a glitter beard.

— Only you can save us! — he cried.

— How? — asked Leon.

The king pointed to an enormous pink button.

**"DO NOT PRESS OR IT WILL GET EVEN MORE CUTE"**

The dragon was already reaching out his paw.

— No, — said Leon.

— But…

— Trust me.

Leon looked at everything around him.

The absurdity.

The bows.

The zombies in tulle skirts.

And suddenly it hit him.

— None of this makes sense.

The king froze.

— What?

Leon narrowed his eyes.

— Too much pink. Too much cute. Too much chaos.

He looked at the dragon.

— This must be a dream.

The dragon blinked.

— …oh.

Leon nodded.

— My dream.

The king began to blur.

The monster truck too.

The zombies waved their bows goodbye.

The dragon squealed:

— Nooo, my pink truck!

Leon closed his eyes.

And said:

— Wake up.

---

He opened his eyes.

He was lying on the deck of "Spark 7."

It was morning.

Everything looked normal.

No pink.

No zombies.

No bows.

He looked beside him.

The dragon was sleeping peacefully…

with a small pink bow on his tail.

Leon sat bolt upright.

— What?!

The dragon opened one eye.

Looked at the bow.

And screamed:

— LEON! I'M ADORABLE!

Leon stared.

The dragon snatched off the bow and frowned.

— Where did this come from?

Leon was quiet for a moment.

Then slowly smiled.

— I think…

it was a slightly real dream after all.

The dragon went pale.

— Were there… monster trucks?

Leon nodded.

The dragon sat up straight.

His eyes went wide.

— We're going back there tonight.

Leon burst out laughing.

And the Storygenerator flickered and displayed:

**"ABSTRACT DREAM COMPLETED."**

Below it added:

**"Cute level: critical."** 🐉🎀🚀✨💖🛻🧟‍♀️
