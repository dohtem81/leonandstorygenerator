Leon and the dragon sat on the deck of "Spark 7."

Things were suspiciously quiet.

Way too quiet.

The dragon was eating pancakes. Slowly. Politely. Without sneaking from the supplies. Without crunching through the plate.

Leon stared at him with growing suspicion.

— Alright. What are you plotting?

The dragon swallowed and looked innocent.
— Me? Nothing.

— That's exactly what worries me.

The Storygenerator suddenly bathed the cockpit in red.

— *ALARM.*

Leon sighed.
— There we go.

A message appeared on the screen:

**"PLANET PANCAKEON VII"**
**"Royal crisis."**

The dragon choked on a piece of pancake.
— Oh no.

Leon narrowed his eyes.
— What "oh no"?

The dragon began nervously swinging his tail.
— Um… a small matter.

— Dragon.

— A really small matter.

— DRAGON.

— …I'm a prince there.

Silence fell.

Leon blinked.
— You're a WHAT?!

— Prince of Pancakeon. Formally the Third Dragon, Heir of the Pan, and Guardian of the Golden Sizzle.

Leon stared at him for a long time.

A very long time.

— You are a… PANCAKE ROYAL?!

The dragon lowered his head.
— It sounds silly when someone says it out loud.

Storygenerator:
— *Sounds very silly.*

— Thanks — the dragon muttered.

---

A few minutes later they landed.

The planet looked like a gigantic kitchen.

Everywhere were golden towers resembling stacks of pancakes.

Fountains poured maple syrup.

The guards had frying pans instead of shields.

Leon actually whistled.
— Okay… this is absurdly beautiful.

The palace doors flew open with a bang.

Out walked a great dragon in a crown.

Enormous.

Dignified.

He smelled of vanilla.

— MY SON! — he roared.

The little dragon beside Leon shrank.
— Oh no.

The king stepped forward and hugged him so tightly that something cracked.

— Dad… my ribs…
